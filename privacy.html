DigitalSaudi

Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

DigitalSaudi ("the App") is a Saudi digital super app built for the Pi Network ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Pi Network username (via Pi SDK authentication only)
  • City preference for prayer times and weather
  • App usage data stored locally on your device

3. Pi Network Integration

DigitalSaudi uses Pi SDK exclusively for authentication and payments. We do not collect passwords, private keys or wallet data.

4. Prayer Times & Location

Prayer times are calculated using the city you select. We do not collect or store your GPS location. City selection is stored locally on your device only.

5. Third-Party APIs

  • Aladhan API — prayer times calculation (aladhan.com)
  • Open-Meteo — weather data (open-meteo.com)
  • The Guardian API — news content

6. Pi Ecosystem Compatibility

DigitalSaudi uses Pi SDK exclusively for all authentication and transactions. No other login methods or payment currencies are supported.

7. No Gambling or Illegal Content

DigitalSaudi does not support gambling, betting, or any illegal activities. All content is compliant with Islamic values and Saudi regulations.

For privacy questions: support.digitalsaudi.pi@gmail.com
